Ninjacart

Revolutionizing the Fresh Produce Supply Chain

Ninjacart is India's largest Fresh Produce Supply Chain Company. We solv one of the toughest supply chain problems of the world by leveraging innovative technology. We source fresh produce from farmers and deliver them to businesses within 12 hours.

With the outbreak of Corona, the traditional classroom based learning has become almost impossible, which laid to the basis of this online learning forum

Technologies used
  • Spring Boot
  • My SQL
  • Mongo DB
  • Vue Js

Prodcuts built at Ninjacart
  • Engineered products across multiple business domains such as Logistics and Operations using microservice architectures.
  • Developed features for On-boarding & Monitoring Logistic Fleets with Real-time Live Location Tracking feature for more than 10000 fleets moving each day thereby reducing the delivery latency by 40% and increased the on-time delivery percentage from 60% to 90%.
  • Developed complete flow of operations for managing the drivers for the company which includes features like Onboarding, Verification, Reporting (Attendance) through facial, Communication, Ticketing and Salary (Cost Calculation) for the drivers.
  • Developed Complete flow for the Last Mile Delivery of goods to the customers which increased the visibility and metrics of On time Delivery, which helped the company to attain more customer satisfaction and User Satisfaction for Drivers.
  • Primary developer for developing backend for company’s internal App (ODIN) used for managing the company’s assets and inventory, interact with Driver and monitor the status of Delivery by the executives of the company.
  • Developed Cost and Tariff Management System for the Last mile.
  • Developed Application for Document Management to create a central repository for effective management, collaboration and tracking, where all the sensitive documents of the company can be stored with no compromise on the authorization providing multiple features like sharing the access, 1 click to edit, setting reminders.
    • Developed Helper micro service (Google Distance) for applications involving tracking of the fleet or drivers for getting real time metrics which reduced huge billing cost and API requests for the company.
  • Primary developer of the dashboard for monitoring the Fleet and the delivery using VueJs.

In summary, what we built is:

  • E-Learning Forum(built in React) allowing students to interact with the experts after the classes, for getting their doubts cleared.
  • Easy way for all the teachers to share the reference material for students, that was used in the class to explain the concepts.
  • Better way for teachers to share the assignments.
  • Quiz for conducting tests for the students
  • Admin Dashboard for managing the student details, onboarding the students and other activities for managing the forum.
