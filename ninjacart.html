Ninjacart

Revolutionizing the Fresh Produce Supply Chain

Ninjacart is India's largest Fresh Produce Supply Chain Company operating in 165+ cities. We solve one of the toughest supply chain problems,by leveraging innovative technology.We source fresh produce from farmers and deliver them to customers within 12 hours.

Transportation of the goods to the customers optimally is one of the key tasks in Logistics.The major challenges in the supply chain are at the Last Mile level, where the goods are transported from the in-house facilites to the customers.

Technologies used
  • Spring Boot
  • My SQL
  • Mongo DB
  • Vue Js

Prodcuts built at Ninjacart
  • Engineered products across multiple business domains such as Logistics and Operations using microservice architectures.
  • Developed complete flow of operations for managing the drivers for the company which includes features like Onboarding, Verification, Reporting (Attendance) through facial, Communication, Ticketing and Cost Calculation for the drivers.
  • Developed features for On-boarding & Monitoring Logistic Fleets with Real-time Live Location Tracking feature for more than 10000 fleets moving each day thereby reducing the delivery latency by 40% and increased the on-time delivery percentage from 60% to 90%.
  • Developed Complete flow for the Last Mile Delivery of goods to the customers which increased the visibility and metrics of On time Delivery, which helped the company to attain more customer satisfaction and User Satisfaction for Drivers.
  • Primary developer for developing backend for company’s internal App (ODIN) used for managing the company’s assets and inventory, interact with Driver and monitor the status of Delivery by the executives of the company.
  • Developed Cost and Tariff Management System for multiple types of trips in the Last mile.
  • Developed Application for Document Management to create a central repository for effective management, collaboration and tracking, where all the sensitive documents of the company can be stored with no compromise on the authorization providing multiple features like sharing the access, 1 click to edit, setting reminders.
  • Developed Helper micro service for applications involving tracking of the fleet or drivers for getting real time metrics which reduced huge billing cost and API requests for the company.
  • Primary developer of the dashboard for monitoring the Fleet and the delivery using VueJs.

In summary, what we built is:

  • Products for Route Generation by optimizing all the key factors to reduce the cost and time to deliver.
  • Products to deliver the goods to the customer and track the delivery enhancing better visibility and metrics.
  • Products to operate the complete flow for drivers and Transport Partners to collaborate with the company.
